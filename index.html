Welcome to Neon Nature, an immersive art experience based at Sheffield's Butcher Works Gallery.


Our exhibition takes visitors on a journey through Earth's natural history in a way that's both educational and interactive. From glowing flowers to luminescent insects, Neon Nature features a range of stunning neon and fluorescent displays that offer a fresh perspective on the natural world. Whether you're a lover of art or nature, Neon Nature is an experience you won't want to miss.


So come and explore, learn, and be inspired by the unique beauty of our planet.

Winged Wonders

An area of the exhibition featuring a variety of bird species in neon and fluorescent displays, showcasing the incredible diversity of bird life on our planet.

The Caves of Illumination

A section of the exhibition recreating the mysterious and fascinating world of underground caves.

The Neon Nature Lab

An interactive area where visitors can learn about the science behind the exhibition and experiment with neon and fluorescent materials.
