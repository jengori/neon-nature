How to find us

On Foot

Neon Nature is located approximately a seven minute walk from the centre of Sheffield, making it a perfect destination for your trip to the city.

By Car

We recommend arriving by foot, bike or public transport but if you wish to arrive by car, live parking space availability is available if you search S1 2NS on Parkopedia

By Train

On exiting the train station follow the steel water sculpture up the slope towards Sheaf Square. Cross the road at the traffic lights, head up Howard Street to the University. Turn left onto Eyre Lane, pass the Red Lion, Butcher Works will be located on your left.

By Bus

Neon Nature is located approximately a six minute walk from Sheffield City Centre Interchange where you can find buses to Meadowhall and all other areas of Sheffield

Frequently Asked Questions

We hope these frequently asked questions will help you plan you visit to Neon Nature and answer any queries you may have. Should you need anything further, please get in touch via our Contact Page

There is a choice of soft drinks, beers, cider and wine in our cafe. Food is served Wednesday to Friday, 5pm to 9.30pm, Saturday and Sunday, 12pm to 9.30pm. We are unable to take bookings.

We have a lift available for visitors who need to use a lift to access the galleries and event spaces on the first and second floors. There are toilets on Level 0 and Level 2. Visit our Access page for full details to assist your visit.

There are a number of accommodation providers within walking distance to Neon Nature, as well as those slightly further out accessible by Sheffield’s public transport or by bike. For more information, visit: Visit Sheffield

Assistance dogs, and assistance dogs in training, are welcome throughout the building. Dogs can come into the Bookshop and Bar but sadly not the Galleries. Water bowls for dogs are available in the café bar on Level 0, just ask and our staff will be happy to help.
